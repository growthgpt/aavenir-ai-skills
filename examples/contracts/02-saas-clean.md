SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of June 1, 2026 (the "Effective Date") by and between Globex Industries, a New York corporation ("Customer"), and SkyData Inc, a Delaware corporation ("Provider").

1. SUBSCRIPTION

1.1 Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the SkyData analytics platform (the "Service") during the Subscription Term.

1.2 The Service is provided as a multi-tenant cloud service hosted by Provider.

2. SUBSCRIPTION TERM AND RENEWAL

2.1 The initial subscription term shall be twelve (12) months commencing on the Effective Date (the "Initial Term").

2.2 This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods (each, a "Renewal Term") unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT

3.1 Annual Subscription Fee: USD 120,000, billed annually in advance.

3.2 Provider shall issue an invoice for each subscription year not later than fifteen (15) days prior to the start of that year. Customer shall pay each invoice within thirty (30) days of receipt.

3.3 Fees may increase by no more than seven percent (7%) at each Renewal Term, provided Provider gives Customer at least sixty (60) days' written notice prior to the renewal date.

4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

4.1 Provider shall maintain monthly uptime of at least 99.9%, measured calendar-monthly excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

4.2 For each 0.1% the monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, Customer shall receive a service credit equal to five percent (5%) of the monthly subscription fee, applied to the following month's invoice.

4.3 Provider shall publish quarterly uptime reports within fifteen (15) days of each calendar quarter end.

5. DATA PROTECTION

5.1 Provider acts as data processor under the Data Processing Addendum attached as Exhibit B, which incorporates the GDPR Article 28 obligations.

5.2 Provider shall notify Customer of any confirmed security incident affecting Customer Data within seventy-two (72) hours of confirmation.

5.3 Provider shall maintain a current list of sub-processors and shall notify Customer at least thirty (30) days prior to engaging any new sub-processor, during which Customer may object on reasonable grounds.

6. AUDIT RIGHTS

Customer may, no more than once per calendar year and on at least thirty (30) days' written notice, conduct or commission an audit of Provider's compliance with this Agreement, at Customer's expense.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information with the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information of like importance, and in no event less than reasonable care, for a period of five (5) years from disclosure.

8. WARRANTIES

8.1 Provider warrants that the Service will perform materially in accordance with the documentation.

8.2 Provider warrants that the Service does not and will not infringe any third-party intellectual property right.

9. INDEMNIFICATION

9.1 Each party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims arising from the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement, gross negligence, or willful misconduct.

9.2 Provider shall additionally indemnify Customer against any third-party claim that the Service infringes such third party's intellectual property rights.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

10.1 EXCEPT FOR (A) BREACHES OF SECTION 7 (CONFIDENTIALITY), (B) A PARTY'S INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, AND (C) GROSS NEGLIGENCE OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, EACH PARTY'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY CUSTOMER UNDER THIS AGREEMENT DURING THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO LIABILITY.

10.2 IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES.

11. TERMINATION

11.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the breach remains uncured.

11.2 Upon termination or expiration, Provider shall make Customer Data available for export in a commonly used machine-readable format for a period of thirty (30) days, after which Provider shall delete Customer Data within sixty (60) days.

12. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict-of-laws provisions. Each party consents to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in San Francisco County, California.
